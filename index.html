Come and join the dazzle of zebras

Welcome to all lacrosse officials: new, current and about-to-be-new. The zebra community is small in comparision to the number of players and we need to increase the number of officials at all levels of lacrosse, so find out how to get involved and develop your officiating skills.

This site is under construction, some information is still being developed, so if you have ideas / requests for content please let us know.

Find an umpire

The umpire register lists the most current information we have, but we rely on individuals and their club / university to let us know of any changes. We are not clairvoyant - so please let us know of any changes as soon as you can.

Become an umpire

There is a Lacrosse Umpire Development Pathway from beginner to international, with Level 2 being the first level beyond simply reading the rules. If you are interested in finding out more, please get in touch.
