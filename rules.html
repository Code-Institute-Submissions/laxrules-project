Lacrosse Scotland Domestic Rules

These are the rules book that will be used for Lacrosse Scotland domestic competition for season 2018/2019. Lacrosse Scotland are not using the new FIL women's rules at present.

Mixed lacrosse games use the women's rules and women's equipment e.g. sticks, but limit the number of men on the field at any one time.
